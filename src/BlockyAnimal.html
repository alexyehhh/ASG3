For my block 3d animal, I made a dog. It is comprised of 21 parts. All parts of each leg are controllable with sliders which include the first, second, and third level joints (legs, feet, and toes respectively). The on/off buttons for the animation makes all the legs move to make it look like the dog is walking. The body, head, legs, and toes are a golden tan color, the ears, nose, feet, and tail are brown, and the eyes are dark brown. The shift click animation causes the dog to stick its tongue out. A user can also click on the screen and drag to rotate the dog as well as using the sliders to do so. I also added a reset camera angle button in case the user wants to reset the camera angle. I used ChatGPT to help me code the functions for mouse dragging control such as mouseDown, mouseMove, and mouseUp.
