Movement:
W: move the camera forward
A: move the camera to the left
S: move the camera backwards
D: move the camera to the right
Q: rotate the camera to the left
E: rotate the camera to the right
You can also use rotate the camera with the mouse by holding and dragging

The correct path to exit the maze is as follows:
1. Go Straight
2. Turn Right
3. Go Straight
4. When possible, turn Left
5. Go Straight
6. At the four way intersection, turn right and go straight
7. When the next left turn is possible, turn left and go straight
8. Turn left and go straight
9. Turn right and go straight
10. When possible, turn right and go straight
11. Turn left and walk straight to the end
You reach the end of the maze when you reach the stone wall.

The two textures I used were stone, for the outer wall, and dirt, for the maze walls. Both of these textures were inspired by Minecraft. For the game, I created a maze, in which the answer can be viewed by clicking the button above. I used Chatgpt to debug my renderfast function and implement the camera movement with the mouse.
